# LifeFresh QuickNote Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 15, 2026

## Introduction

LifeFresh QuickNote respects your privacy. This application is designed to help users manage leads, reminders, reports and customer information securely.

## Information We Collect

- Lead information entered by the user.
- Reminder information.
- App settings and preferences.
- Backup files created by the user.

## Data Storage

All CRM data is stored locally on your device using a secure local database.

We do not upload your CRM data to our servers.

## Advertising

This application uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google may collect advertising identifiers and diagnostic information according to Google's Privacy Policy.

https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Permissions

The application may request:

- Internet
- Notifications
- Storage access
- Phone call permission (only when you choose to call a customer)

These permissions are used only for application functionality.

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no electronic storage method is 100% secure.
